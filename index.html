Republic Day - India's Journey

Celebrating India's Progress from 1950 to Present

1950 1952 1957

1950 - Adoption of the Indian Constitution

India adopted its Constitution and became a republic on 26th January 1950.

More Information about 1950

This year marks the foundation of India's republican democracy with the adoption of the Constitution, a pivotal moment in India's journey.

1952 - First General Election

India held its first general elections to elect its leaders, with Jawaharlal Nehru becoming the first Prime Minister.

More Information about 1952

This was a significant year in India's history, marking the first democratic elections where millions of people voted for the first time.

1957 - Launch of India's First Satellite Project

India's first satellite project, Aryabhata, was launched in 1957.

More Information about 1957

This event marked the beginning of India's space exploration program, setting the stage for significant achievements in the field of science and technology.
